My Skillset

Psychotherapy Lens

I have been in the social work field for 14 years and practicing therapy for 9 years. I see empathy as genuine curiosity without judgment. When someone feels understood, they feel felt and that leads to powerful insight not only as a therapist but also as a researcher and designer.

Effective Communicator

My clinical training makes me highly skilled in observing subtleties of non-verbal and verbal communication. My job relies on the strategy of how to communicate with different types of people.

Competitive Researcher

Research is the most important part of understanding and defining a problem. I enjoy discovering what challenges users are facing and what attempts to solve the problem they have made and coming up with a solutions-focused plan that meets the user's needs.
